## PRODUCER NOTES

This ad positions Vespera as **the successor to OnlyFans**, not a competitor. The messaging emphasizes:
- **Ownership** (your audience, your brand, your data)
- **Fairness** (50/50 split, transparent)
- **Technology** (AI agents, automation, modern stack)
- **Community** (team support, not going solo)

Avoid explicit content in visuals — keep it aspirational and professional. The target viewer is a 22-35 year old creator who's frustrated with platform cuts and wants to level up.
